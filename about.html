Our Story

SG Multi Tools started as a small project to create useful online utilities. What began with just a few calculators has grown into a comprehensive collection of 20+ tools serving thousands of users daily. We continuously improve our tools and add new features based on user feedback.

Today, we're proud to help people around the world with their everyday needs, from calculating ages and converting currencies to generating secure passwords and encoding URLs.
