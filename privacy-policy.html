Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

1. Information We Don't Collect

SG Multi Tools is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information. All tools run entirely in your browser – no data is sent to any server.

2. Local Storage

Some tools (like Note Text Saver) use your browser's local storage to save your notes. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to us. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: For typography. Google may collect usage data as described in their privacy policy.
  • Font Awesome: For icons. No data collection.
  • GitHub Pages: Hosting service. See GitHub's privacy policy for details.

4. Cookies

We do not use cookies. Some third-party services (like Google Fonts) may set cookies – please refer to their privacy policies for more information.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at contact@sgtools.in.
