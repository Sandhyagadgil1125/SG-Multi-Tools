About the Word Counter

Our Word Counter is a comprehensive text analysis tool that gives you instant statistics about your writing. Whether you're a student working on an essay, a writer crafting content, or a professional preparing a report, this tool provides valuable insights into your text, including word count, character count, sentence count, and reading time estimates.

‚ú® Key Features

  • Comprehensive metrics: Tracks words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, paragraphs, and unique words.
  • Reading time: Estimates how long it takes to read your text (based on 238 words per minute).
  • Speaking time: Estimates how long it takes to speak your text (based on 150 words per minute).
  • Real-time updates: All statistics update instantly as you type or paste text.
