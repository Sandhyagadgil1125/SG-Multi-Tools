The SGTech Vision

Why SGTech?

SGTech Multi Tools was built to solve the privacy gap in online utilities. Most websites upload your personal files or text to a server. We changed that. Our platform acts as a shell that runs powerful code directly on your hardware.

Privacy Architecture

We utilize browser-based JavaScript modules. Whether you are using the Image Compressor or the Password Generator, the computation happens in your RAM, not on our cloud. This makes us immune to data breaches.

Our Commitment

We commit to keeping these 20 tools free, ad-lite, and high-performance. We believe utilities should be instant and friction-free, requiring no logins or tracking cookies.
