About Case Changer

This tool helps you convert text between different cases instantly. It's perfect for:

  • Writers & Editors: Format titles, headings, and content
  • Developers: Convert variable names between naming conventions
  • Students: Format essays and research papers
  • Social Media: Create stylized text for posts

All processing happens in your browser - your text is never sent to any server.
